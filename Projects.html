Projects our Team has Created

CognitION

This project focuses on our voyage into the world of AI chat bots. Inspired by the increased interest in the development of AI and the popularity of ChatGPT, CognitION is our own attempt at creating a sentient chatbot that can help with day to day activities and provide knowledge answers to whatever you question.

Ars Bellica

This project consists of testing the capabilities of a digital mind and seeing how it adapts to human strategies. This is an AI specialized in playing various board games. This project was inspired by chess bots being so advanced, a human can not beat them. We decided to branch out to other board games, to allow Ars Bellica to create strategies and test whether they work, in order to be perfect at the games played. Some popular games it plays are Risk, Monopoly, and Settlers of Catan.

Wellness

This project is an AI that is focused on helping you become the best version of yourself without having to pay for fitness coaches and dieticians. This AI will provide you with fitness plans and meal suggestions based on whatyour endgoal is. It will also adapt its recommended plans based on your response to them. This will help anyone have access to the resources in order to live a healthy life on their terms, without having to pay for expensive apps or coaches.
